Saket Gupta
Partner, Co-Founder
saketg@kuerassociates.com

Qualification: B.A.LLB (hons. in Constitutional Law) from National Law University Odisha.

Saket holds a superior knowledge of Constitution related matters, including service disputes. He has a vast experience in criminal cases, which includes economic offences and corruption cases. His other areas of practice lie in property disputes, essential commodities, matrimonial and family disputes. He has the experience of appearing before Patna High Court, Debt Recovery Tribunal, various District & Sessions Courts, various District Consumer Forums and Bihar State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission.

Shantanu Braj Choubey
Partner, Co-Founder
shantanub@kuerassociates.com

Qualification: B.A.LLB (hons. in Corporate Law) from National Law University Odisha.

Shantanu has a wide array of expertise in matters related to corporate and banking law. He has an extensive experience in commercial contracts, due diligence, consumer disputes and Alternative Disputes Resolution including arbitration. He appears regularly before Patna High Court, various District & Sessions Courts, Debt Recovery Tribunal, various District Consumer Forums and Bihar State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission. In addition to the above, he has worked under several legal luminaries in Supreme Court, Jharkhand High Court, Madras High Court, Karnataka High Court and Jabalpur High Court. He has presented his research in several conferences including ICLAE, 2016, organized by IIT Kanpur, IIM Ahm and GNLU, and YSI Plenary, 2016 held at Budapest, Hungary. At present, he is on leave to pursue his LLM in Comparative Law, Economics and Finance at IUC Turin, Italy.
